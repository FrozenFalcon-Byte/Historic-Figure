Career as Scientist

After graduating from the Madras Institute of Technology in 1960, Kalam joined the Aeronautical Development Establishment of DRDO as a scientist. During his early career, he was involved in the design of small hovercraft, and remained unconvinced by his choice of a job at DRDO.

Presidency

On 10 June 2002, the National Democratic Alliance, nominated Kalam for the post of the President of India.

Post-Presidency

After leaving office, Kalam returned to teaching, and became a visiting professor at various institutions.
